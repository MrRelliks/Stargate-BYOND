Welcome to the OU Cyber Security Society

We are a collective of students with an interest in Cyber Security from the Open University. This is a collaborative project to build a secure website. We have students from all walks of life, some working full time and studying part time and others doing the reverse. What brings us all together is our passion for learning.

This website is being built by students for students.
